company history

Location>> home > company history

CYM utilizes manufacturing and packaging facilities in areas favorable to both production and shipping. This concept maximizes logistical support and minimizes costs to the customer.

  • 2010

    CYM underwent organizational changes to its global operations, resulting in a more efficient business model, extending from manufacturing all the way to product delivery.

  • 2009

    CYM consolidated all manufacturing, packaging and sourcing operations to C&C Shanghai Plastic & Metal Company in Shanghai to centralize the management force for more efficient production and cost savings.

  • 2008

    CYM announces that it has consolidated its 3 Nanjing factories into a single facility near Nanjing's Lukou International Airport, southeast of Nanjing. The move to this new location will benefit customers by bringing CYM's Nanjing nail plant, manufacturing, packaging and sourcing operations into a centralized location, improving efficiencies and reducing costs.

  • 2006

    Grand Opening of CYM's fourth Chinese factory, C&C Shanghai Plastic & Metal Company in Shanghai. The 500,000 square foot facility contains state-of-the-art Progressive Stamping Presses, Plastic Injection Molding machines, Automated Packaging Equipment and many other advanced manufacturing capabilities.

  • 2005

    Opened Zhongshan Packaging & Logistics Center for sourcing South China suppliers.

  • 2003

    Opened offices in Osaka to enter the Japanese market for OEM and DIY items.

  • 2000

    Opened CYM Nail Factory in Nanjing.

  • 1997

    C&C International Group, established in 1997 to act as the central management unit for the CYM family of companies. The function of C&C International Group is to service customers and prospects in the United States, Canada and Latin America.

  • 1995

    Opened second factory in Nanjing, Crown Top Industrial Corp (CT). CT manufactures a wide range of OEM products including metal stampings, light forgings and castings. In addition to Rigging hardware CYM expanded its product categories to include Hinges and Fasteners.

  • 1992

    Built first CYM factory in Nanjing, China, QiangJue Development Hardware Company (QJ). Installed Packaging Lines and sophisticated Quality Control Laboratory with Pull Test machines and a Rockwell Hardness machine. Established the CYM Export Department in Nanjing to service the European

  • 1991

    Established first China presence in Tianjin, Hebei Province, providing Hand Tools and Rigging Accessories to American and European markets.

  • 1981

    CYM Industrial Corporation, a Global Manufacturing and Trading Company, founded in Kaohsiung, Taiwan in 1981.
