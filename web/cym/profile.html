COMPANY PROFILE

CYM MISSION STATEMENT
“To Provide quality Manufacturing, Packaging and Trading, Goods and Services to the Global OEM, ODM and DIY Marketplace”.

For over 31 years, CYM Industrial Corporation has been a leading Global Manufacturing and Trading company, specializing in OEM, ODM and DIY Builder’s Hardware products.

Our corporate offices in Los Angeles, California manage over 250 production and staff members located at CYM facilities in Shanghai, China and Kaohsiung, Taiwan.

Core competencies include

Metal Stamping, Rigging Hardware, Wire Forming Products, Plastic Items, Packaging & Labeling, Logistic Services

CYM provides a professional Engineering team and an experienced Quality Assurance staff. This guarantees that products will be of a high quality and made to your specifications.

An experienced English-speaking Customer Service group rounds out the complete CYM product offering. Financially strong and willing to co-invest with the right partner; CYM is the total solution for your manufacturing needs.
