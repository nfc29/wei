ABOUT CYM

With the Corporate Office in Los Angeles, CA, USA our multilingual staff works closely with our customers for understanding requirements.

CYM employs over 250 production and staff employees. Trading and manufacturing facilities are in Shanghai, China and Kaohsiung, Taiwan.

CYM ensures on time shipping and competitive pricing.

We are also willing to co-invest with our partners in the development of new products.

CYM’ s Engineering team and Quality Assurance staff ensure quality at all levels of production. This guarantees that products will be made to customer specifications.

CORE COMPETENCIES INCLUDE

Metal Stamping

Progressive die stamping for high volume efficiency, conventional stamping and forming for products that require a low cost tooling investment.

Hot Forging and Cold Heading

Forging of standard Eye Bolts or unique custom designs.
Cold Heading for Nails, Screws and unique custom pins.

Rigging Hardware

Chain Hooks and Fittings, Turnbuckles, Shackles and Wire Rope fittings.

Wire Forming Products

Hooks and Eye Bolts, U-bolts, Peg Hooks, Nails, Wire Racks and Custom wire formed product designs.

Plastic Items

Injection Molding, Insert Molding, Extrusion and Blow Molding.

Assembly, Packaging & Labeling

Dedicated Assembly Lines with In-Process inspection.
Packaging using Boxes, Cartons, Blister Packs, Clamshell, Skin Cards, Shrink Wrap, Poly Bags, Hanging Cards and UPC Labeling.

Logistic Services

Experienced Logistics Team provides all export documents, schedules and tracks shipments.

Quality Control Inspection and Testing

CYM’s Quality Assurance Engineers are highly trained to ensure that our production process and quality assurance procedures meet your requirements. We employ Process Control procedures based on your specific requirements.

  • Incoming material is qualified before it is released for production.
  • Production operators are trained and responsible for producing items that meet customer specifications before the product is moved to the next processing step.
  • Roaming inspectors check products at every step in the production process.
  • Periodic calibration of all measuring instruments and gauges.
  • Go-No-Go gauges.
  • 6 Sigma process control.
  • Pre-control techniques where we adjust the process based on strict easy to understand rules that assure close tolerance production.
  • Complete detailed inspection.
  • Capability studies to verify our production process is capable of meeting customer requirements.
  • Final inspection before shipment.
    From pre-production planning through every step of production and shipping, CYM’s QA Engineers make sure our products meet customer requirements.
